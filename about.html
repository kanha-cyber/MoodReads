Each mood tells a story. Each story paints a feeling. Between the pages lies a world that mirrors your emotional landscape—whatever it may be. MoodReads connects the chapters of your heart with the stories that need telling.

Our Mission

MoodReads was born from a simple truth: we don't always read for information—sometimes we read to feel. To escape. To process. To dream. We believe the best book recommendations aren't based on algorithms alone, but on the emotional landscapes we traverse.

Our mission is to create a sanctuary where readers can explore stories through the lens of their current emotional state, discovering books that resonate with their present moment—whether seeking the warm embrace of romance, the intellectual thrill of dark academia, or the boundless imagination of fantasy worlds.

How It Works

1. Choose a Mood

Begin by selecting an emotional atmosphere that speaks to your current state. Each mood has been carefully crafted to capture a distinct reading experience.

2. Discover Curated Selections

Explore thoughtfully chosen books that align with your selected mood, each one promising to complement or transform your emotional journey.

3. Save Your Favorites

Keep track of books that resonate with you, building a personal library that reflects the many versions of yourself.
